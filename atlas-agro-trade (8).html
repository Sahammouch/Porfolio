🛒 For Buyers

Can you handle the specific requirements of my market? ▾
Yes. We have documented expertise in Middle Eastern Gulf standards (halal compliance, SFDA/Saudi regulations, GCC conformity), European Union regulations (EU pesticide MRLs, GlobalG.A.P., traceability requirements), and North American standards (FDA prior notice, USDA-APHIS, FSMA food safety). We tailor all documentation and quality controls accordingly.
What are your typical transit times? ▾
From Moroccan ports: Southern Europe: 2–3 days; Northern Europe: 5–7 days; Middle East Gulf ports: 10–14 days; US East Coast: 10–12 days. Transit times may vary based on vessel schedules and routing. Air freight is also available for urgent, premium shipments.
What Incoterms do you offer? ▾
We offer FOB (Casablanca/Tangier Med Port), CIF to major ports worldwide, CFR, EXW, and DDP for established buyers with the right import infrastructure. All terms are Incoterms® 2020 compliant. If you're unsure which suits your situation, contact Mr. Hammouch for advice.
How do you ensure quality for long-distance shipments? ▾
We use temperature-controlled refrigerated (reefer) containers, conduct pre-shipment quality inspections at origin, work exclusively with freight forwarders specialising in perishables, and provide real-time shipment tracking. Any quality claims must be submitted in writing with photo documentation within 5 business days of receipt.
What payment terms do you accept? ▾
We accept Letter of Credit (L/C), advance wire transfer (T/T), and other arrangements agreed in writing on our proforma invoice. Bank account details are exclusively provided on official invoices issued by Mr. Sidi Achraf Hammouch. We never share banking details via email or WhatsApp outside official invoices. Proforma invoices are valid for 15 calendar days.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)? ▾
MOQs vary by product, market, and season. We typically work with full container loads (FCL) — 20ft or 40ft reefer containers. LCL (less than container) shipments may be accommodated for established clients or on specific products. Please share your required volumes with Mr. Hammouch for a tailored response.

📦 Products & Quality

What products do you trade? ▾
We specialise in four core categories: Citrus Fruits (oranges, clementines, lemons, grapefruits), Fresh Vegetables (tomatoes, bell peppers, green beans, potatoes), Exotic Fruits (avocados, mangoes, passion fruit), and Berries & Grapes (strawberries, table grapes, blueberries). Primarily Casablanca Port and Tangier Med Port in Morocco. Tangier Med is Africa's largest container port with direct connections to Algeciras, Rotterdam, Hamburg, and Le Havre. We also coordinate shipments from other African ports (e.g., Dakar, Abidjan, Mombasa) depending on the source country.
What export documents do you provide? ▾
Standard documentation includes: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading (or Airway Bill), Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, and Health Certificate. Additional documents such as GlobalG.A.P. certificates, halal certificates, or USDA-required phytosanitary documentation are available upon arrangement.
Can you arrange air freight for urgent shipments? ▾
Yes. For time-sensitive or premium produce, we can arrange air freight via Mohammed V International Airport (Casablanca) to any global destination. Air freight costs and lead times are confirmed on inquiry. Contact Mr. Hammouch to discuss your specific needs.

📄 Proforma Invoices

What is a Proforma Invoice?

A proforma invoice is a preliminary document issued by Atlas Agro Trade SARL prior to shipment. It details the product specifications, quantities, unit prices, total value, Incoterms, and payment terms agreed between both parties. It serves as the formal basis for your purchase order and payment arrangement.

Validity & Conditions

  • Valid for 15 calendar days from date of issue
  • Subject to product availability and seasonal conditions
  • Prices reflect market rates at the time of issue
  • Re-quotation required after expiry
  • Not a binding contract until both parties confirm in writing

What's Included

  • Full product description, grade, and packaging
  • Unit price and total value (USD or EUR)
  • Incoterms (FOB, CIF, CFR, etc.)
  • Port of loading and destination
  • Payment terms and deadline
  • Bank account details (on final confirmed invoice only)

How to Request

To receive a proforma invoice, submit an inquiry specifying your product requirements, volume, destination port, and preferred Incoterms. We will respond within 2–3 business days.
